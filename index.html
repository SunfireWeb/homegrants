North Central Washington Home Grants

Free Down Payment Assistance

Having trouble saving for the down payment to purchase a home? Good news! There may be free down payment assistance available to you in the form of a grant (meaning you don't have to pay it back).

If you are looking to purchase a home in north central Washington (stretching from Chelan and Manson through Entiat, out to Leavenworth, and down into Wenatchee or East Wenatchee and Rock Island), and could benefit from a grant or other assistance to help with the down payment, you've come to the right place. Take a few minutes to complete our form with as much detail as you feel comfortable, and you'll be contacted by our approved mortgage professional within 2 business days to further discuss the loan and grant qualification process.
 

The following 10 questions are all optional - feel free to skip any of which you are unsure. None of this information is stored anywhere. Omission of answers will not affect your chances, however you may still be asked to provide this information during the qualification process.
